The UC Riverside Perception, Action, and Development Lab studies:
  • - Natural visual experiences of infants, children, and adults in everyday tasks
  • - Visual exploration of complex scenes and natural environments
  • - Visual guidance of motor skills like reaching, walking, and crawling
  • - Perceptual recalibration to changing possibilities for action

Interested in participating in studies? If you have a child under 12 and would like more information about visiting the lab, please visit childstudies.ucr.edu or email ucrchildstudies@gmail.com to learn more.

Interested in joining the lab? Interested undergraduates should complete an RA application. Potential graduate students: Please visit the psychology department page for admissions information, and feel free to contact me with any questions.
